**Cost:** Free (fully grant-funded)  
**Requirements:**
- Released from Oregon state prison within 20–270 days
- Willing to engage with case management and training
- Eligible for services pre-release and 12+ months post-release

**Life-Impact Weight:** 9/10  
**Priority Index:** 9.3  
**[HIGHEST PRIORITY - Comprehensive Support]**

**Services Provided:**
- Resume/cover letter writing
- Interview preparation
- Career mapping and planning
- Job search and direct placement assistance
- Computer skills training
- Financial literacy and budgeting
- Housing assistance resources
- Case management (pre- and post-release)
- Wraparound services (mental health, substance abuse treatment partnerships)

**Action Plan:**
1. **Step 1:** Contact your releasing prison's WorkSource staff (typically 90–180 days pre-release)

5. **Step 5:** Receive job placement assistance and ongoing support
6. **Step 6:** Access wraparound services (housing, training, financial support) as needed
