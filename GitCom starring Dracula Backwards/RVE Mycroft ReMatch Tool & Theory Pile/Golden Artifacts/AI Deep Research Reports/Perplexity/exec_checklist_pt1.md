## IF YOU MISS A SESSION

**Week 1-2:** One missed session = immediately reschedule (makeup next day if possible)

**Week 3+:** 
- One missed session/week = acceptable (schedule makeup later in week)
- Two missed sessions/week = deload immediately (recovery needed)
- Three+ missed sessions/week = reassess program (injury? life situation?)

**General rule:** Never miss more than 1/6 sessions per week. Never skip more than 1 week in 12.

If you miss more than allowed:
1. Don't quit
2. Acknowledge the disruption
3. Make up the session ASAP
4. Return to schedule
5. Continue tracking
6. Deload following week if needed

## IF YOU PLATEAU

**Plateaus are normal at:**
- Week 4-5 (first deload effect)
- Week 8-9 (between peaks)
- Week 11 (final adaptation period)

**If plateau occurs unexpectedly:**
1. Check sleep (often insufficient sleep is culprit)
2. Check nutrition (often insufficient protein)
3. Check progression rate (may need +2.5 lbs instead of +5 lbs)
4. Check consistency (missed sessions?)
5. Take deload week anyway (recovery fixes most plateaus)

**Most plateaus resolve with sleep + nutrition fix. Don't panic.**
