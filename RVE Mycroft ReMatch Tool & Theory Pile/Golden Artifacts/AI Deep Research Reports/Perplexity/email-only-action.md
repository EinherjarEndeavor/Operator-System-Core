## EMAIL WRITING TIPS

✓ Keep subject lines clear and specific (mention "research," "study," or "substance use")
✓ Be honest about medical history (it's actually an asset for addiction research)
✓ Mention "opioid/methamphetamine use disorder in remission" (shows you're stable)
✓ Say you're on "medication-assisted treatment" (shows compliance)
✓ End with clear call-to-action: "When can I schedule screening?"
✓ Include phone number (studies prefer phone contact)

✗ Don't mention nicotine use unless asked
✗ Don't apologize for substance use history
✗ Don't ask "will this pay?" in initial email (ask at screening)
✗ Don't mention money/compensation as primary motivator
✗ Don't attach documents (unless asked)
