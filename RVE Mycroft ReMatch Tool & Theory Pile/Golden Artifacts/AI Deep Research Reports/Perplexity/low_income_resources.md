## KEY TAKEAWAY: "Free Money" Tax Credits

Two major Oregon programs give you cash back at tax time **even if you owe zero income tax**:

1. **Oregon Kids Credit**: $1,000–$5,000 (depending on number of children ages 0–5)
2. **Oregon EITC**: Additional $1,000+ (working families)

**File your 2024 taxes** (deadline April 15, 2025) to get these refunds. Even if you made $0 in income, you may be eligible.

## SNAP Benefits Note

As of **October 1, 2025** (FY2026), maximum SNAP benefits:
- **Single person**: $295/month
- **Family of 2**: $535/month
- **Family of 3**: $765/month
- **Family of 4**: $994/month

**If you are a SNAP recipient**, you automatically qualify for:
✓ Oregon Lifeline (free/discounted phone & internet)  
✓ WIC (if you have children <5 or pregnant)  
✓ Head Start (if you have kids 0–5)  
✓ ERDC childcare subsidy (if working/in school/on TANF)  
✓ Medicaid (likely)  
✓ Many other programs listed above
