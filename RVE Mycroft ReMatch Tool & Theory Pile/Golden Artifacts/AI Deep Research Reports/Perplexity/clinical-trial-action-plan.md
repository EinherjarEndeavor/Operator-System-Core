- **Expect payment timing:** $250–$300 per visit (varies). Most studies pay after each visit or at end of study via prepaid card, check, or gift card.

## IMPORTANT NOTES

### Eligibility Edge Cases You Should Know
1. **Buprenorphine/Suboxone + Research:** Some opioid use disorder trials **exclude** people on MAT; others **prefer** them. Always ask: "Are people currently on buprenorphine eligible?"
2. **Nicotine Use:** Do not mention unless asked directly. Nicotine use is common and usually not an exclusion criterion (unlike illicit drug use).
3. **Prior Substance Use History:** Your history is an **asset**, not a liability, for SUD-focused research. Emphasize "in remission" and "on stable MAT."
4. **Recovery Status:** Being in early recovery (3+ months) is often a requirement. You are 3+ months sober and on stable meds—this is ideal.

### Red Flags to Avoid
- ❌ Do NOT claim to be a "veteran" if you aren't
- ❌ Do NOT lie about current medications or SUD history
- ❌ Do NOT miss screening appointments (they may not reschedule)
- ❌ Do NOT ask about "cutting corners" on screening (they can disqualify you)
- ✅ **Do** ask about all eligibility details upfront by email to save time

### If You Get Rejected
- Ask: "Is there a similar study I would qualify for?"
- Ask: "When can I reapply if I don't qualify now?"
- Move to next institution on list immediately
