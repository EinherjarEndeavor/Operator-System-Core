## CRITICAL CLARIFICATION #1: JAIL vs PRISON RELEASE DIFFERENCE

**This matters for program eligibility.**

### Jail Release (Your Situation)
- County-level facility
- Typically shorter sentences (under 1-2 years, varies by jurisdiction)
- Release to Washington County probation/supervision
- Usually involves county-level reentry services
- Community-based support more immediate

### Prison Release (Original Dossier Context)
- State/federal facility
- Longer sentences (years to decades)
- Release to state parole supervision
- State-level reentry services
- Often requires more intensive support structure

**The distinction matters:** Some statewide programs explicitly require prison release status. Some explicitly welcome jail release. We've removed the inapplicable ones.

**Why This Is Your Top Priority:**
