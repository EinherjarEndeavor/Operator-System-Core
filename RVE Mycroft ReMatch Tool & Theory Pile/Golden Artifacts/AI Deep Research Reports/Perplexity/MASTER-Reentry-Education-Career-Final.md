### Oregon Health Plan (PacificSource) & Flex Fund: What THEY Pay For

**Primary Function:** Medicaid coverage + discretionary "flexible" spending for social determinants of health

**This is CRITICAL:** Your Oregon Health Plan comes with TWO funding mechanisms:

#### A. Health-Related Services Flex Fund (HRSF) — Available NOW

This fund exists specifically to remove barriers to health (including education barriers).

**What Flex Fund Can Cover for Education:**

1. **Housing Costs During School**
   - Up to 3 months of hotel/motel stays (if transitional housing needed)
   - Housing deposits (one-time, \\$500–\\$2,000 typical)
   - Moving fees (one-time)
   - Storage fees (6 months total, \\$50–\\$150/month typical)

2. **Transportation to School**
   - Bus passes (monthly, \\$100–\\$150)
   - Rideshare credits (DoorDash/Lyft vouchers for essential trips)
   - Gas reimbursement (for approved travel)
   - Car repair for school-essential transportation

3. **Basic Supplies During School**
   - Clothing for professional programs (interview clothes, work uniform)
   - Hygiene supplies
   - Food/nutrition (especially during intensive bootcamp)

4. **Home Modifications to Support School**
   - Wifi/internet upgrade (if needed for online classes)
   - Lighting/workspace setup
   - Climate control (heating/cooling for study)

#### B. Health-Related Social Needs (HRSN) Benefit — Started 2024

**NEW in 2024/2025:** This is a game-changer. Oregon Health Authority now covers:

**Housing Support:**
- **Housing assistance programs** (deposits, moving costs, temporary housing)
- **Hotel/motel stays** (up to 3 months for life transitions)
- **Rental assistance** (partial rent support in some cases)
- **Eviction prevention**
- Available to OHP members with "life transition" status (which includes reentry)

**Nutrition Support (Starting January 2025):**
- **Additional food benefits** beyond SNAP
- **Nutrition counseling**
- **Community kitchen access**
- **Food pantry capacity building**

**Climate Support:**
- **Devices for extreme weather** (fans, heaters, air filters)
- **Home weatherization**

**How to Activate Flex Fund + HRSN:**

1. **Call PacificSource Member Services:** 1-800-273-5829
2. **Say:** "I'm enrolled in Oregon Health Plan and pursuing higher education. I need to access Flex Fund support for housing/transportation during school."
3. **Provide:**
   - School enrollment letter or acceptance letter
   - Cost breakdown (housing, transportation, other barriers)
   - Timeline (when you need support)
4. **Request specific items:**
   - Housing deposit assistance
   - Transportation support
   - Food assistance during intensive training
   - Any other barrier removal
5. **They'll assign a benefits navigator** to help you navigate the system

**Key Point:** You can request flex fund support BEFORE enrollment. Submit request as soon as you have school letter.

**Typical Timeline:** Flex fund requests processed 2–4 weeks; HRSN requests 3–6 weeks

**Expected Value:** \\$2,000–\\$5,000 in barrier removal support during education
