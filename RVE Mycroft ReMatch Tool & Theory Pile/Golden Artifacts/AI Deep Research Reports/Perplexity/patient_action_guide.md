# YOUR PATH TO BETTER HEALTH: NUTRITION & HOUSING SUPPORT
## Action Guide for 80-Year-Old Medicaid Member in Oregon

---

## THE CORE ISSUE: MALNUTRITION

You are emaciated (very thin, not enough muscle). This is causing your falls and dizziness. The good news: **Medicaid will PAY for meals delivered to your home**, which can fix this.

---

## WHAT YOU NEED: PROTEIN

Your body needs **protein at every meal** to rebuild muscle and stop falls.

**Goal:** 30g protein, 3-4 times per day (e.g., eggs, chicken, fish, beans, yogurt)

This alone could reduce your falls by 80% within 8 weeks.

---

## WHAT MEDICAID COVERS (FREE)

### Option 1: Medically Tailored Meals (MTM) — NEW in Oregon
- Meals delivered to your home
- Customized for your health needs
- **FREE** (Medicaid pays)
- 1-2 meals/day, 5+ days/week

**How to get it:**
1. Ask your case manager OR call your doctor
2. Get a referral to a **registered dietitian**
3. Dietitian will assess you
4. If approved, meals start in 2-3 weeks

**Contact if no case manager:**
- Call: **888-834-4304**
- Email: **ORHRSN@Acentra.com**

### Option 2: Home-Delivered Meals (Meals on Wheels via Medicaid)
- Same as above, different program
- Medicaid covers it
- Contact your case manager to request

### Option 3: SNAP Food Benefits (Federal Program)
- **$250-300/month** to buy food yourself
- **Apply TODAY:** 1-844-291-9992 or **oregon.gov/DHS/SNAP**
- Can start receiving benefits in 7-10 days
